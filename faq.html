What is Wraiths DAO?

We believe that a decentralized, permissionless and open metaverse can be the future. We don't know exactly what that will look like - yet. WraithsDAO is an initiative to be part and at the forefront of that movement. For now, having skin the game is our move in that direction. We invest in NFTs projects and communities that we think are set to play a role.

Very few Wraiths can claim that they are natives to this plane... ⩪

Wraiths DAO allows members to come together and find the best way to direct capital towards value accretion through a voting and consensus mechanism.

How are the DAO funds managed?

All funds are invested/spent following a consensus agreement, where each Wraith has the right to participate. For large sums exceeding 100k, the DAO uses a multi-sig wallet infrastructure that requires multiple administrator approvals to ensure the security of the funds.
