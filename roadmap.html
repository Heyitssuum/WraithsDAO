RoadMap

Wraiths can see images of the past, the current or the future... ⩪

  • Era I Drop completed

    Era I is the genesis Wraiths drop of the DAO. It includes 4 original Wraiths (1x Executioner, 1x Necromancer, 1x Occultist, and 1x Dark Knight).

  • Era II Drop Ongoing

    Era II is the second Wraiths drop of the DAO. It includes 8 original Wraiths (2x Executioner, 2x Necromancer, 2x Occultist, and 2x Dark Knight).

  • MetaDex premium airdrop Incoming

    A MetaDex pass will be airdropped to all Era I and Era II Wraiths holders. This pass gives access to all premiums features on MetaDex.

  • Era III Drop Incoming

    Era III is the third Wraiths drop of the DAO. It includes 8 original Wraiths (3x Executioner, 3x Necromancer, 3x Occultist, and 3x Dark Knight).
